## Manual-Only Verifications

| Behavior | Requirement | Why Manual | Test Instructions |
|----------|-------------|------------|-------------------|
| `duel` player hit confirm is clear | GF-01 | Needs real-time visual judgment | Start `duel`, fire each weapon at AI, confirm center feedback appears immediately on player hits and is distinct from miss |
| kill confirm is stronger than normal hit | GF-02 | Relative intensity is experiential | Land a killing shot in `duel`, confirm center feedback is visibly stronger and survives into any killcam transition |
| `environment_hit` does not impersonate `player_hit` | GF-01 | Requires comparing visual language | Shoot crates / cover / barrels and verify muted confirm appears, but it is weaker and visually distinct from player hit |
| `smg` remains readable in `deathmatch` | GF-03 | High-frequency readability is perceptual | Enter `deathmatch`, hold fire with `smg`, confirm center HUD does not become noisy or opaque |
| `shotgun` aggregates to one primary confirm | GF-03 | Needs observing one trigger with multiple pellets | Fire `shotgun` at close range and confirm one main center confirm per trigger, not one per pellet |
| `near-miss` only happens on true miss | GF-01 | Requires behavior sequencing | Fire shots that narrowly miss and then direct hits; confirm only miss cases trigger the dramatic near-miss effect |
| reset paths clear stale confirm state | GF-01, GF-02 | Requires flow transitions | Test reload, weapon switch, round reset, pause, killcam exit, and rematch; confirm no stale confirm overlay persists |
